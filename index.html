How it works
A fresh RSA-OAEP 2048-bit keypair is generated for this delivery only. Files are encrypted with AES-GCM-256. The private key is locked with your passphrase via PBKDF2 (200k rounds). Send the HTML file, then share the passphrase via a separate channel.
⚠ Share the passphrase via a separate channel — not in the same email or message as the file.

Quick Guide
CLIENT-SIDE CRYPTOGRAPHIC SIGNING
🔏
Step 1 of 5
Sign, watermark, and trace
ShadowSign lets you send cryptographically signed copies to multiple recipients. Every copy has a unique fingerprint. If it leaks, you know exactly who had it — all in your browser, zero servers. Supports PDF, PNG, DOCX, XLSX, and 🎬 MP4/MOV video.
🪪
Step 2 of 5
Choose your Identity
Your identity signs every document and stores your send ledger. Pick whichever fits your setup.
🖥️
Web3 Wallet — Desktop (Recommended)
Install MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, Brave, or Rainbow as a browser extension. Click Connect in the Identity Setup modal. Already connected? Use the ⇄ Switch button on the Identity tab to change wallets.
📱
Web3 Wallet — Mobile
Open ShadowSign inside your wallet app's built-in browser (MetaMask → Browser, Coinbase → DApp Browser). File uploads may be unreliable in wallet browsers — RSA Identity is more stable on mobile.
📂
RSA Identity — Works Everywhere
Generates a keypair in-browser. Download the .shadowid file — you must keep it to preserve your send ledger between sessions. Best choice for mobile users.
📦
Step 3 of 5
Delivery Options
When you add a recipient, you can choose how to deliver their signed file.
📄
Standard File
Downloads as a normal PDF, DOCX, or Image. Optionally wrap in a Passphrase Delivery file after.
🦊
Web3 Delivery
Downloads as an encrypted HTML file. Only the recipient's exact ETH Wallet address can unlock and extract it.
🔍
Step 4 of 5
Verification
If a file ever leaks, go to the Verify tab and drop it in. ShadowSign will read the steganography and metadata, cross-reference it with your loaded Identity Ledger, and tell you exactly which recipient it belongs to.
🎬
Step 5 of 5
Video Watermarking
Drop any MP4, MOV, AVI, WebM, or MKV file and ShadowSign burns a visible diagonal watermark + QR stamp directly into every frame. The watermark survives screenshots, re-encodes, and phone-photographs.
🎨
Frame Watermark
Your watermark text is drawn diagonally across every frame at your chosen color and opacity — identical to the PDF/image overlay.
📋
Signature Bar + Manifest ZIP
Enable "Embed signature block" to add an HMAC bottom-bar on every frame and bundle the video with a plaintext certificate in a ZIP.
⚠️
Browser Encoding
Processing happens entirely in your browser via MediaRecorder. Long videos use real-time play mode; short clips use seek mode. Output is MP4 (Chrome/Edge) or WebM (Firefox/Safari).
